My Pokemon Hobby Page

I have collecting pokemon cards and playing the games since I was a little kid. To me it is super fun because it actually forces you to think in order to beat the other player. You have to actually try to use the correct moves and make sure you are not a typing that the other player is resistant to. Not only that but collecting the cards is awesome because if you get valuable cards you can sell them for money, plus they look super cool.
